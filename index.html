Our Story

SahneBySaba was born from a passion for creating jewelry that combines timeless elegance with contemporary design. Each piece is handcrafted by skilled artisans using ethically sourced materials.

We believe jewelry should tell a story - your story. That's why we create pieces that are as unique as the individuals who wear them.

Based in San Jose, California, we serve clients worldwide who appreciate fine craftsmanship and sustainable luxury.
